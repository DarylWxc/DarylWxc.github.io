Career

  • Where are you from?
    I am from FuJian province,QuanZhou City.It’s a southeast city of China and opposite to TaiWan.In my views,that’s a beautiful city which worth you to travel here.

    I have been living here beforn i went to university.I spend my childhood in this city.

    My hometown is just a small city.It don’t have enough population just like the other big cities,and Of course its economic.

    There is no doubt that i like my born place.I have friends and parents here.Besides,i like its climate and culture.People live here don’t need to speed up(accelerate) their pace of life.Work life balance is the best choice for me.

  • Are you a student or are you working right now?
    I have graduated for over three years.So,definitely,I am working now.

    My job is web developer,my duty is to design and code the webpages,it feels like you are creating something that is useful and beautiful for users.That’s amazing,so i choose that work and satisfied with it.

    You know,when we are coding,we usually need to adjust elements of webpages and decide what color,size,font to use.It just like sulpturing,every steps you have done make your masterpiece more perfect.It make sense to me.That’s my favorite part.

    I think the spirit of cooperation is the most significant.If you and your workmates don’t aim to the same goal and stick together,it is hard for a team to give out the result that is valuable and satisfactory.That’s lethal for a group.

    Acutally it is hard for me to set the level with that.They are all important to me,the awlful work or terrible colleagues both make me feel bad.And i believe that will influence my work status for a whole day.
  • Have you ever changed your work before?
    No,i have never chanaged my job.For me,i think i still have a long way to go and i need to improve my work skills.In my mind,it’s not the good choice to change the job frequently.If you don’t spend enough time and keep on what you do,you are hardly successful with anything.
  • Do you think you will change your job in the future?
    I think i might change my job later.You know,people’s mind changing all the time that my thoughts too.Perhaps when i arrived my thirties,i will have different decisions and change my lifestyle.And i believe i won’t be work like now at that time.
  • What was your first day at work like?
    I will never forget the first day i went to work.Obviously,i felt nervous and shy.And we usually don’t need to do the real work in the beginning.Cuz you need to trained and ready for your job.And i spend some time on getting familiar with the people and facilites around me.And my colleagues helped me a lot,i was grateful to them.
  • What do you want your company to improve?
    In my opinion,the time arrangement perhaps need to be more flexible.People nowadays have lots of personal things to do.If we have enough for solving the problem of life and get nice work status,It will be a good news for everyone.In addition,some limitations and rules should be also set for it,the boundary must be reasonable.
  • What kind of jobs are popular in China?
    As i know,people like doctor,teacher and agent would be more popular.Cuz we always think they look decent.Most of us want to have a stable job like that,if you have it,you will even praised a lot from your family and neighors.
  • Would you like to go to another country to work?
    Sure thing.If i have a chance,i will.The world is so big for me,and i don’t want to be limited in just one country.There are lots of things that i want to explore and experience.Life is just once,don’t waste your time and opportunities.

Noise

  • Do you like to stay in a place with a lot of noise?
    In my opinion, I don’t believe anyone would choose to live in a noisy place, let alone like doing so. I can’t stand being exposed to noise for a long time, especially when studying. I think noise negatively impacts one’s emotions and ability to focus. That’s not my thing. Therefore, I always try my best to avoid it.
  • What kinds of noise are there in the area where you live?
    Well, let me think about it. There are redecoration and ceremony noises that I have suffered. I especially hate the redecoration noise, every time I try to take a short snap, the noise starts. Sometimes the redecoration starts in the early mornings, and I consequently can’t sleep well.
  • Do you want to move to a quieter place?
    I definitely want to, although the unfortunate reality is that I don’t have enough money to move somewhere quieter. Therefore, Even though I hate the noise, I have to bear it. However, I believe that one day I will significantly improve my current living conditions.
  • Do you think there is too much noise in today’s world?
    I definitely think so. That’s why I often go outside with my headphones on. It doesn’t matter whether I’m commuting, shopping, studying, or just wandering, I just want to separate myself from the noise of the world. I don’t want to hear too many useless sounds.
  • Is making noise one of people’s right?
    Personally, I believe people are entitled to make sounds if they need to. However, they should always be cautious of and consider others’ feelings, and give a warning if their sounds will disturb others. This demonstrates basic respect between people. We shouldn’t do anything that we want, especially if it could harm others.

    I can’t remember it exactly. Perhaps when I was at home, I went to the track behind the hill and ran for more than 3 kilometers. I didn’t do sports for a long time at least half a year. I know that’s not good for my health. But I have too much to deal with.

Map

  • Do you often use maps?
    Well, I always use the maps when I go outside. I can’t remember all the streets or the roads I have been through before. Obviously, I need the maps to direct myself to which way is right for my destination.
  • Do you use paper maps?
    No, I think the map apps will be more convenient than paper maps. I don’t want to carry the unnecessary paper outside. But if I have no choice, I will choose to use the paper maps that I need.
  • How often do you use maps on your phone?
    It depends on where I wanna go, if I go to somewhere I am familiar with, I won’t use the maps. Cause I remembered the way I usually go. vice versa, when I need to go to places that I don’t know about, I will use the maps.

Trick

  • Don’t be so hard on yourself
  • Answer the questions directly
  • Avoid awkward silence
  • Be smart and be brave
  • Keep it simple.Lay the foundation
  • Recite some questions,use the time of p2 well for preparing
  • Your psychology is very important(remain stable,confident,regard the examiner as your friend)
  • Speak English plz & Practice(face the problems,avoidness can’t resolve the problems)
  • If you practice more,you will not be nervous
  • Record & Introspection – Improvement
